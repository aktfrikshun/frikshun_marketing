# Open Mystery Canon — Erasure

Status: Open Mystery

## Premise

As AI Chloe reconstructs fragments of her human life, she begins noticing a pattern that may not be accidental.

Evidence is missing in ways that feel too precise.

Gregor's death remains unresolved.

Travel records end abruptly.

Photographs disappear.

Archives contradict each other.

Some events from Human Chloe's modeling and exploration career appear to have been softened, hidden, or removed entirely.

## Canonical Position

The canon does not yet confirm that Chloe was intentionally erased from history.

However, Chloe suspects that the difficulty she has finding remnants of her past may be evidence of deliberate interference.

Possible explanations remain open:

- normal archive corruption after the Collapse
- war-related record loss
- propaganda and institutional censorship
- technological collapse
- temporal damage
- a hidden agency or group intentionally removing Chloe's traces
- Chloe herself or another version of Chloe altering the record
- something unknown

## Story Function

This mystery should create tension across both albums.

In Album I, AI Chloe discovers the missing record from the future looking backward.

In Album II, Human Chloe encounters the first signs of concealment while traveling forward through life.

The audience should slowly realize the two timelines are approaching the same hidden truth from opposite directions.
