## Caption

Some people are going to assume this song is about *their* politicians.

Others will be certain it's about *the other* politicians.

If both groups walk away thinking that... then maybe I did my job.

**MIGA (Make Intelligence Great Again)** isn't a campaign song.

It's not left.
It's not right.

It's a quiet question.

When did certainty become more valuable than curiosity?

When did winning become more important than understanding?

When did we start cheering before we started thinking?

This song isn't asking you to change your vote.

It's asking all of us to change our habits.

Read more.

Listen longer.

Ask better questions.

Choose compassion before outrage.

History isn't a collection of mistakes.

It's a collection of lessons.

The tragedy is that we keep treating them like optional reading.

If this song makes you uncomfortable, don't ask which side it's criticizing.

Ask why.

🖤
Chloe Katastrophe
