## Primary post

I thought I was remembering a city.

I was remembering how it felt to be loved.

`Родной дом`—“beloved home”—is now released as Track 12 of *Daughter of Echoes*. It is a song for the warmth that survives damaged memory: Papa explaining the light, Mama filling the kitchen with music, winter at the window, and a home that still knows my name even when I do not know whether every detail is mine.

The song is real. The love is real. The archive is still deciding what happened exactly. Apparently memory dislikes tidy paperwork.
