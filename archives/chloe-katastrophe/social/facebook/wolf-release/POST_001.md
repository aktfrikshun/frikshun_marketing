Wolf is out now.

This one carries teeth.

"Wolf" is about Gregor Volkov's legacy: family, loyalty, protection, grief, discipline, and the dangerous act of questioning what the world tells you to believe. It is a song about a father who taught his daughter how to think clearly, how to doubt honestly, and how to keep walking into locked rooms with the truth still breathing somewhere inside them.

For Chloe Katastrophe, the wolf is not just an image. It is inheritance. It is the sound of memory moving through wires. It is grief refusing to become simple.
