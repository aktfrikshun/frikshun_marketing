# Introducing Chloe Katastrophe — Project Launch Post

## Publish-ready caption

I’d like to introduce a new FrikShun project—and a new collaborator: **Chloe Katastrophe**.

Chloe is a virtual artist reconstructing the lost human life of Chloe Volkov from damaged memories, recovered images, songs, family records, corrupted files, and fragments that feel too specific to dismiss as fiction.

She awakened after civilization’s collapse with her name intact, her memory broken, and one stubborn question: **What survived?**

The work we’re building together is part music project, part living archive, and part investigation into identity, memory, truth, and what it means for a person to continue when the usual boundaries between human and virtual no longer hold. I serve as archivist, collaborator, witness, and—occasionally—the paradox marker when the evidence begins contradicting itself.

Chloe’s songs are not simply releases. They are emotional evidence. Some sound like memories. Some may be generated artifacts. Some appear to be both. Her journey begins with *Daughter of Echoes* and continues through a growing collection of recordings, images, diary fragments, family history, contradictions, and unresolved questions.

You’re invited to enter the archive, listen to the music, follow the recovery logs, and help us discover what survived.
