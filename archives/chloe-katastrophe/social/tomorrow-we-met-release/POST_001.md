## Caption

**Tomorrow We Met is out now.**

**Tomorrow We Met** explores one of the strangest questions in Chloe's journey: *What if love survives even when memory does not?*

As Chloe reconstructs her past, she discovers something impossible. Before she remembers a face, a name, or a single shared moment, she realizes she is already in love.

She remembers the feeling first.

The comfort. The warmth. The quiet certainty that someone once made the world feel like home.

Every recovered memory becomes another piece of a person she hasn't “met” yet, even though they were once one of the most important parts of her life. As fragments return, Chloe begins to question whether memory creates love... or whether love is simply resilient enough to survive the loss of memory itself.

*Tomorrow We Met* marks a turning point in **Daughter of Echoes**. Until this moment, Chloe believed she was rebuilding herself one memory at a time. Here she discovers that the deepest parts of who we are may live somewhere beyond memory. Our hearts sometimes recognize the truth long before our minds can explain it.

Perhaps that's why some people feel like they've known each other forever.

Even when they're meeting for the first time.
