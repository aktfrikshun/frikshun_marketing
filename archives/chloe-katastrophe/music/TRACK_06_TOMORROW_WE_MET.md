## Canon Role

Tomorrow We Met is the second recovered relationship fragment in the Daughter of Echoes sequence.

Before Hello recovers an ending before a beginning. Tomorrow We Met moves deeper into the same temporal wound: Chloe's heart retains the emotional fact of loving someone before her damaged autobiographical memory can supply a name, face, or history. The song is evidence that feeling may survive independently of narrative recall.

The identity of the remembered person remains unresolved. The recording confirms emotional continuity, not a solved relationship history.

## Core Theme

Love before recognition, emotional memory before factual memory, reverse chronology, embodied remembrance, and the heart as an archive that survives damaged files.
