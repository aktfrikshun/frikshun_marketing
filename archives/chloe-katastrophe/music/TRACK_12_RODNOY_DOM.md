## Release Role

`Родной дом` is Chloe's testimony that origin can survive as a feeling before it survives as evidence. Its remembered home is made from Gregor's patient curiosity, Isabella's expressive warmth, ordinary friendship, and the childhood certainty that love did not need to be proven before it could be trusted.

This is not Gregor's martyr song and not a political elegy. Here, he is Papa. The track gives *Daughter of Echoes* one last room of human warmth before its title track asks what Chloe is becoming.
