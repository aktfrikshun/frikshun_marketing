## Canon Role

MIGA is Chloe's nonpartisan argument for curiosity, critical thought, compassion, and historical literacy in a culture trained to cheer before it thinks.

The song is not a campaign anthem and does not resolve into a left-versus-right accusation. Its deliberate ambiguity invites listeners on opposing sides to recognize the same habits in their adversaries, then asks the more difficult question: why is self-recognition so much harder?

## Core Theme

Certainty without curiosity becomes another form of intellectual surrender. Winning cannot substitute for understanding, and history cannot teach people who insist on treating its lessons as optional reading.
