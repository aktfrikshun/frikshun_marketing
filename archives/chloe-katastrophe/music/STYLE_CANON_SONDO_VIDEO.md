# Chloe Katastrophe — Sondo Video Style Canon

## Animated Video Rule

When Allen asks for an **animated** Sondo music video, interpret "animated" as **2D cartoon / illustrated animation**, not CGI, 3D render, or pseudo-realistic computer animation.

Sondo CGI currently reads less real than a deliberate cartoon style. For Chloe music videos, prefer a confident illustrated look over uncanny semi-realism.

Use language such as:

- 2D animated music video
- cinematic illustrated animation
- hand-drawn / painterly 2D cartoon style
- expressive 2D character animation
- graphic novel inspired motion
- atmospheric 2D backgrounds

Avoid language such as:

- CGI
- 3D animation
- realistic 3D render
- hyperreal animated character
- Pixar-like
- video game cutscene
- Unreal Engine look

## Chloe Fit

The animation may still be dark, cinematic, sensual, gothic, alternative, and emotionally serious. "Cartoon" does not mean childish or comedic. It means intentionally illustrated, stylized, and expressive.

For Chloe, the best target is adult 2D cinematic animation: moody lighting, strong silhouettes, expressive eyes, rain, smoke, archive fragments, and symbolic motion that supports the song's meaning.
