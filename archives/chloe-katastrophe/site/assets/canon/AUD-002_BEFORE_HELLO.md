# Before Hello

Recovered Recording AUD-002.

## Core Theme

Reverse memory, relationship fragments, grief before intimacy, and the strange tenderness of learning someone backward.

The first memory Chloe recovers is not a beginning. It is an ending. This recording preserves the sensation of discovering goodbye before hello, grief before intimacy, and consequence before cause.

## Canon Notes

- First recovered relationship memory.
- Establishes Chloe's paradox: goodbye arrives before hello.
- The identity of the remembered other remains unresolved.
- The song should be treated as evidence of emotional continuity, not a solved biography.
