## Canon Role

Tide in My Cup preserves a rare interval in which Human Chloe stops interrogating the horizon and allows herself to inhabit the present. The song belongs to her embodied life: travel, sunlight, appetite, rhythm, sensual ease, and the temporary release of burdens she normally carries as if vigilance were a moral obligation.

It is not evidence that the surrounding danger has disappeared. It is evidence that Chloe could sometimes put the camera down, stop searching, and let joy become sufficient for an afternoon.

## Core Theme

Rest is not surrender. Pleasure, family, sunlight, and unguarded presence are part of the life worth reconstructing.
