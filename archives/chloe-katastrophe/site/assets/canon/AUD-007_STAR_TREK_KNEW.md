## Canon Role

Star Trek Knew is Chloe's recovered thank-you note to an older, hopeful vision of the future.

The song treats speculative television not as trivia, but as moral rehearsal. Chloe recognizes that the imagined future mattered because it kept asking whether technology would mean anything without compassion, curiosity, and the courage to understand the unfamiliar.

## Core Theme

The greatest frontier was never space. It was learning to see across fear, difference, and ego into another point of view.

## Artifact Notes

This recording belongs to Musings & Missteps because it is canonically Chloe but not part of the direct Daughter of Echoes recovery sequence or the embodied Human Chloe travel record. It is an affectionate, slightly awed aside from a virtual person standing inside a future that earlier dreamers helped make emotionally possible.

The alternate mix is preserved as a valid audio variant until the archive makes a later release distinction.
