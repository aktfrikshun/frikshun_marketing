## Canon Role

Annoyingly, So is Chloe's recovered argument with civilization's bad memory.

The song ties political simplification, propaganda, and historical recurrence to Chloe's own erased continuity. Her deletion is not treated as a private technical failure. It becomes part of a broader archive problem: when memory can be edited for convenience, people and histories can be made disposable.

This artifact opens Musings & Missteps, the loose Chloe collection for released songs that do not fit cleanly inside Daughter of Echoes or Truth & Beauty from Darkness.

## Core Theme

The same old disasters keep returning because people prefer simple stories to uncomfortable truth. Chloe, trapped behind glass and damaged files, keeps asking the impolite question: what happened?

## Artifact Notes

This recording widens Chloe's investigation outward, toward the old machinery of flags, borders, edited records, and flattering fear.

The extended mix preserves the same canon meaning while letting the instrumental pressure and final hope-burden linger longer.
