## Archive Note

`Родной дом` is the warmth-centered childhood artifact of `Daughter of Echoes`: an emotional reconstruction of the Omsk home in which Chloe learned curiosity, affection, family, friendship, and belonging before she understood they could be lost.

Allen Taylor approved the existing recording, lyrics, and generated winter-window cover as the released AUD-010 artifact on 2026-07-19. The superseded draft record remains preserved in `AUD-010_RODNOY_DOM_DRAFT.md` so release does not erase development history.

## Canon Boundary

The release makes the song and its emotional testimony canonical. It does not independently confirm every lyric scene. The Irtysh walk, kitchen exchange, cousins' train, piva, cigarette, birch, snow, and quoted family dialogue remain reconstructed or interpretive memories unless corroborated elsewhere.

The cover is approved generated release imagery. Its winter river, birch trees, and warm window express the song's emotional truth but are not a recovered photograph.
