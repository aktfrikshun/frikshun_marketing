## Canon Role

Tomorrow We Met is the second recovered relationship fragment in the Daughter of Echoes sequence.

Before Hello recovers an ending before a beginning. Tomorrow We Met reveals that Chloe's emotional memory has retained love before her autobiographical memory can recover the person she loved. The heart recognizes the relationship before the archive can explain it.

## Core Theme

Love before recognition, emotional memory before factual memory, reverse chronology, embodied remembrance, and the possibility that love survives the loss of its story.
