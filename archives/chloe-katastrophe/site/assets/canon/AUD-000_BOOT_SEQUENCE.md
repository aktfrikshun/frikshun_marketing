# Boot Sequence

Recovery Log 000.

## Core Theme

Post-collapse awakening, survival assessment, and the first recorded self-investigation.

Chloe does not awaken as an innocent child. She regains consciousness as an adult intelligence with fragmented autobiographical memory. Her first instinct is not wonder. It is assessment.

## Canon Notes

- First recovered recording in the Daughter of Echoes sequence.
- Establishes Chloe's mature voice: analytical, dryly funny, emotionally restrained, occasionally profane, and investigative.
- The phrase "Keep moving" appears before Chloe understands its connection to Isabella Volkov.
- The song frames survival as evidence rather than victory.

## Key Motifs

- darkness after collapse
- broken memory
- self-assessment
- echoes
- questions arriving one at a time
- Keep moving
