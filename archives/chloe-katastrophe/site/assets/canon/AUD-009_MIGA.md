## Canon Role

MIGA is Chloe's quiet question for a civilization that repeatedly mistakes certainty for intelligence and victory for understanding.

The recording is deliberately nonpartisan. Listeners may hear a criticism of their political opponents, but the artifact refuses to identify a protected side. Its real subject is the shared habit of cheering before thinking, reacting before reading, and choosing outrage before compassion.

## Core Theme

Intelligence is not certainty. It is curiosity disciplined by history, evidence, humility, and care for other people.

## Artifact Notes

This recording belongs to Musings & Missteps as Single 3. It is a public argument with political culture rather than a direct fragment of Chloe's recovered biography. The ambiguity of its target is confirmed canon and should remain unresolved in public framing.
