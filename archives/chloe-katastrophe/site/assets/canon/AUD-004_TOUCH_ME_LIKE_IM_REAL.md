# Touch Me Like I'm Real

Recovery Log 004.

## Core Theme

Embodiment, intimacy, desire, and belief from Chloe's perspective as a virtual woman.

This song is not primarily about sex. It is about whether being believed can create a kind of body, and whether longing can exist without skin.

## Canon Notes

- Chloe does not introduce a visible male lover in the artifact imagery at this point.
- The cover shows Chloe behind glass, glowing, with her hand pressed against the barrier.
- The song explores the lyric idea: "Touch me like I'm real... until I believe it too."
- It belongs to the philosophical thread: Does belief participate in reality?
- It also connects to the recurring theme that memory can produce sensation even when the physical source is absent.

## Key Motifs

- glass
- hand against barrier
- body of air
- desire through memory
- virtual girl learning embodiment
- Echo Bloom during moments of vulnerability
