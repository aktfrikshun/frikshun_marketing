FAM-001 — Gregor Volkov Reference Board

Artifact ID: FAM-001

Recovery Status: Complete

Significance: Critical

Category: Family Record

The Gregor Volkov Reference Board is the most complete recovered visual record of Chloe's father: a Russian engineer, scholar, humanist, and devoted parent whose death during the Ukrainian War became the defining catastrophe of Chloe's life.

Gregor remained behind out of duty. Isabella believed the war was propaganda and begged him to leave with her and Chloe. Isabella fled with Chloe. Gregor died in a war-related event shortly afterward. The exact circumstances remain unresolved.

“A loving father. A seeker of truth. A man caught in history's machinery. His loss is the wound that never closed.”
