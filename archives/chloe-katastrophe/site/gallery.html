Family Photographs

The Volkov family records are among the most emotionally significant areas of the archive. The Gregor-and-Chloe image anchors Chloe's later search for truth, sacrifice, and memory, while its precise historical provenance remains subject to archive review.

Confirmed Family Canon

Gregor Volkov

Russian scientist and systems engineer. Gentle, analytical, quietly funny, and skeptical of propaganda. He remained to protect critical civilian infrastructure and the people depending on it—not to fight, and not because he trusted the state.

Confirmed Family Canon

Isabella Volkova / Volkov

Italian heritage. Passionate, empathetic, protective, and morally defiant. She got Chloe to safety because survival for their daughter came before institutions or abstract principle. Later American records use the flattened surname Volkov.

Confirmed Identity Canon

Chloe Volkova / Volkov

Raised from Gregor's questions and Isabella's fire. American records later flatten Volkova to Volkov; after Gregor's death she chooses Katastrophe as remembrance, warning, and defiance.
