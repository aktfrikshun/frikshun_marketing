Petition for Recovered Artifacts

Chloe’s memory is incomplete. If you have found a photograph, recording, journal fragment, timestamp, transmission, dream, rumor, contradiction, or impossible coincidence connected to Chloe Katastrophe, Gregor Volkov, Isabella Volkov, FrikShun, or the Continuity Project, submit it for review.

Do not submit private information about real people without permission. The archive accepts fictional, artistic, historical-canon, and in-universe artifacts only.

Until the secure submission endpoint is active, send artifact leads to chloekatastrophe@gmail.com.

How Chloe Reads the Archive

Each artifact is treated as a possible memory state. Some are direct records. Some are symbolic. Some may be decoys, corrupted echoes, or intentional signals placed for Chloe to find later.

  • Complete artifacts are clear enough to preserve as historical records.
  • Partial artifacts contain missing metadata, damaged context, or uncertain origin.
  • Contradictory artifacts are not discarded. Chloe studies them as evidence of timeline drift.
  • Echo artifacts appear before they should exist.

Signal Protocol

Some clues may appear outside this site: buried in captions, metadata, lyrics, social posts, SoundCloud comments, image titles, hidden pages, or broken links. These are not mistakes. Chloe calls them breadcrumbs from the future.
