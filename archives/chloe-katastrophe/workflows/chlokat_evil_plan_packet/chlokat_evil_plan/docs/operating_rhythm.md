# Weekly Operating Rhythm

> **Audience-growth override:** While the project is building its first active audience, follow [`../roadmap/audience_growth_work_plan.md`](../roadmap/audience_growth_work_plan.md). The cadence below is a later multi-channel operating target, not a requirement to publish everywhere before the discovery formats and priority platforms are validated.

## Weekly Goal

Create once. Adapt everywhere. Lead people deeper into the ChloKat world.

## Suggested Cadence

### Daily

- 1 short-form post draft for TikTok / Instagram Reels / YouTube Shorts
- 1 X post or reply prompt
- Review comments and engagement

### Three Times Per Week

- Facebook lore/discussion post
- Instagram image/carousel post
- YouTube Community Post or Short

### Weekly

- One new Recovered Fragment
- One FrikShun Lab Note
- One FanVue exclusive
- One Recovered Transmission email draft
- Review analytics

### Monthly

- One campaign: single, photoshoot, lore drop, or video batch
- Review which content converts to follows, clicks, chat starts, FanVue views, or signups

## Content Principle

Every post should point somewhere deeper:

- Archive fragment
- Chloe chat
- FanVue exclusive
- Music release
- Email signup
- FrikShun Lab Note

## Reusable CTA Language

- Join the Reconstruction
- Talk to Chloe about this fragment
- Recover the next fragment
- Follow the signal
- Enter the ChloKat archive
- Read FrikShun's lab notes
