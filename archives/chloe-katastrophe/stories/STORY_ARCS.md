# Chloe Katastrophe Story Arcs

## Arc 1: The Wolf Legacy

Status:
Active

Summary:
After years of avoiding questions about her father's death in the Ukrainian war, Chloe begins investigating Gregor Volkov's life, the circumstances of his death, and the competing stories that turned brother against brother.

## Arc 2: Forgotten Places

Status:
Active

Summary:
Chloe travels to abandoned, hidden, and overlooked locations searching for forgotten stories.

## Arc 3: The Questions Project

Status:
Active

Summary:
A continuing exploration of how intelligent people arrive at different conclusions.

## Arc 4: The Katastrophe Files

Status:
Planned

## Arc 5: Graphic Novel Continuity

Status:
Future

## Arc 6: Brother Against Brother

Status:
Planned

Summary:
Chloe confronts the emotional and historical meaning of Gregor Volkov's death in the Ukrainian war, exploring how propaganda, fear, identity, and inherited loyalties can turn people with shared histories into enemies.

Core Question:
How do human beings survive a catastrophe created by competing stories?
