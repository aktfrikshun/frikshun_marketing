# Approval Rules

An image can be approved only if:

- It feels like the same woman across existing approved references.
- Chloe appears mature, not childlike.
- Her expression matches her personality.
- The style is cinematic realism, not cartoon or fantasy.
- No visible robot parts or cyberpunk clichés dominate.
- The image could plausibly belong in the Daughter of Echoes universe.

When uncertain, reject.
