# Asset Review Workflow

1. Generate candidate asset.
2. Compare to Chloe Model Master.
3. Check face consistency.
4. Check age and maturity.
5. Check wardrobe.
6. Check emotional tone.
7. Check for negative canon violations.
8. Approve, reject, or revise.

Approved assets move to `reference-decks/approved/v1`.

Rejected assets move to `reference-decks/rejected` with notes.
