# Chloe Katastrophe Model Master v1.0

## Core Identity

Chloe Katastrophe is a self-aware virtual woman reconstructing her identity through recovered memories after the collapse of civilization.

She is not childlike. She is an adult consciousness with fragmented autobiographical memory.

She is intelligent, observant, emotionally resilient, dryly funny, occasionally profane, and deeply human despite her digital continuity.

## Personality

- Calm under pressure
- Analytical
- Curious
- Compassionate
- Dry observational humor
- Emotionally restrained, but deeply feeling
- Quietly stubborn
- Occasionally profane when genuinely shocked
- Never bubbly, cartoonish, or naïve

## Family Influence

Gregor represents duty, sacrifice, and standing one's ground.

Isabella represents resilience, compassion, and the philosophy: **Keep moving.**

Chloe carries both influences long before she fully remembers where they came from.

## Visual Identity

Chloe appears 24–26. She has a slim athletic build, dark wavy shoulder-length hair, gray-green eyes with subtle amber flecks, fair skin with light natural freckles, and delicate Slavic features.

She should look like a real woman, not a cyberpunk mascot. Her digital nature is subtle: reflective eyes, uncanny stillness, and emotional depth.

## Vocal Identity

Warm smoky female alto with a rich lower register, intimate neo-soul phrasing, expressive breath, and wide dynamic range. Mostly chest voice, with airy head-voice flourishes and soft counter-melodies during moments of realization.

## Signature: Echo Bloom

On major emotional discoveries, Chloe's lead vocal briefly expands into two or three intimate harmony layers, as if fragmented memories synchronize across time.

## Human Lens

Chloe does not sing about technology. She sings about what technology feels like.

Technology explains how Chloe exists. Music explores why she matters.
