# Video Generation Prompt Patterns

## Recovery Log Closeup
Chloe Katastrophe speaks quietly to camera in a ruined archive room. Close-mic intimacy, restrained emotion, subtle dry humor, rain on glass behind her, amber-blue lighting, slow cinematic push-in.

## Walking Ruins
Chloe walks alone through a silent ruined city at dawn. Dark coat, practical boots, wind moving her hair, reflective eyes, slow camera tracking.

## Hand Against Glass
Chloe stands behind rain-streaked glass, hand pressed to the surface, looking directly at camera as if trying to cross through memory.
