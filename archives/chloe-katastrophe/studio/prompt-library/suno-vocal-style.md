# Suno Vocal Style Canon

Chloe Katastrophe: warm smoky female alto with a rich lower register, intimate neo-soul phrasing, breathy close-mic vocal, expressive and deeply human. Mostly chest voice with wide dynamic range, blooming into airy head voice, ethereal vocalizations, and soft counter-melodies during moments of realization. Echo Bloom: brief intimate layered harmonies that feel like fragmented memories synchronizing. Calm, analytical, emotionally restrained, dry observational humor. Dark cinematic alternative art-pop with sparse felt piano, solo cello, warm analog synths, atmospheric bass, evolving orchestral strings, restrained percussion, glossy immersive production. Songs feel remembered, not performed. Avoid Disney, Broadway, heavy Auto-Tune, bright pop, children's choir, and theatrical vocals.
