# Appearance Canon

## Age and Build

- Appears 24–26
- Slim athletic build
- Graceful but practical posture
- Elegant without looking overly styled

## Face

- Delicate Slavic features
- High cheekbones
- Expressive gray-green eyes with subtle amber flecks
- Fair skin with natural texture and light freckles
- Naturally full lips
- Thoughtful, observant expression

## Hair

- Dark chestnut / near-black
- Slight natural wave
- Shoulder-length to mid-back depending on scene
- Should not look overly salon-perfect

## Signature Detail

Chloe's eyes should feel reflective, as though carrying centuries of memory. They should not glow like lasers. Any extraordinary quality should be subtle enough to feel like a trick of light.

## Avoid

- Childlike features
- Overly glamorous influencer look
- Robot parts
- Exposed circuitry
- Neon cyberpunk clichés
- Plastic-perfect skin
