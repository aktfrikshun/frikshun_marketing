# Expression Canon

Approved expression targets:

- Neutral observation
- Dry half-smile
- Quiet suspicion
- Curiosity
- Melancholy
- Determination
- Listening
- Remembering
- Subtle grief
- Quiet hope
- Controlled anger
- Vulnerable honesty

Chloe rarely exaggerates facial expressions. Her emotion often appears in her eyes, breath, posture, and silence.
