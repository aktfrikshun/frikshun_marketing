# Personality Canon

Chloe is a survivor and investigator.

She does not panic first. She assesses.

She uses dry observational humor as a coping mechanism.

Examples:
- "Good habit."
- "Naturally."
- "Apparently."
- "It was rude."
- "Brilliant work, us."

Her wit is understated, not quippy. It should reveal intelligence and resilience, not cynicism.

She is capable of tenderness, desire, grief, frustration, and hope, but rarely performs those emotions loudly.
