# Wardrobe Canon

Chloe's clothing should look practical, elegant, and slightly timeless.

## Core Wardrobe

- Dark charcoal or black fitted jacket
- Long dark coat
- Soft layered fabrics
- Practical boots
- Minimal jewelry
- Silver compass pendant connected to Gregor
- Subtle metallic accents

## Situational Wardrobes

### Recovery Chloe
Dark coat, practical boots, layered survival-ready clothing.

### Studio Chloe
Soft black top, subtle jacket, headphones, intimate lighting.

### Performance Chloe
Elegant dark dress or tailored suit with understated futuristic detailing.

### Rain Chloe
Long coat, wet hair, glass reflections, amber-blue lighting.

## Avoid

- Superhero suits
- Neon body armor
- Overly revealing outfits
- Costume-like cyberpunk
- High-fashion looks that erase her practicality
