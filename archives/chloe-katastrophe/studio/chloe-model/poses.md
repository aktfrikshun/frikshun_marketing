# Pose Canon

## Signature Poses

- Hand pressed against glass
- Looking through rain-streaked glass
- Walking alone through ruins
- Holding an old photograph
- Sitting at an archive console
- Listening with head slightly tilted
- Looking upward at damaged sky
- Standing still while the world feels broken around her
- Singing close to a microphone
- Reading a recovered journal

## Body Language

Restrained, alert, thoughtful, never overly posed. She should look like she is inhabiting a moment rather than modeling for the camera.
