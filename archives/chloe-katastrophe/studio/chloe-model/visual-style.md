# Visual Style Canon

## Mood

Dark cinematic realism with emotional intimacy.

Think recovered footage, ruined elegance, quiet science fiction, and post-collapse atmosphere.

## Palette

- Steel blue shadows
- Amber highlights
- Ash gray
- Rain-wet glass
- Warm practical lights
- Muted violet / dusk tones

## Recurring Motifs

- Glass barriers
- Rain
- Dust
- Ruins
- Reflections
- Archive rooms
- Old photographs
- Faint sunrise
- Hands touching surfaces
- Broken city skylines

## Camera Language

- 35mm environmental shots
- 50mm natural portraits
- 85mm intimate closeups
- Shallow depth of field
- Slow, deliberate framing
- Cinematic but grounded
