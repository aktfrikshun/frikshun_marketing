# Voice Canon

## Singing Voice

Warm smoky female alto with a rich lower register.

Mostly lower and middle registers, grounded in chest voice.

Wide dynamic range used for expression, not vocal athleticism.

Airy head-voice flourishes, ethereal vocalizations, and soft counter-melodies appear during moments of realization.

## Performance

She rarely sounds like she is performing. She sounds like she is remembering.

Breathy close-mic delivery. Natural pauses. Subtle hesitation. Emotionally authentic.

## Echo Bloom

On emotionally significant words, Chloe's lead vocal briefly expands into two or three soft harmony layers. These represent fragmented memory states synchronizing across time.

Use sparingly.

## Avoid

- Broadway delivery
- Heavy Auto-Tune
- Diva belting
- Theatrical vibrato
- Robotic processing
