# Chloe Katastrophe Brand Guide

## Public Promise

Gothic Beauty.
Dangerous Curiosity.
Independent Thought.

## Name Usage

- Formal public name: Chloe Katastrophe
- Approved nickname / merch mark: ChloKat

ChloKat may be used on branding materials, fan merchandise, social graphics, stickers, apparel, and compact visual marks. It should feel familiar and playful without replacing Chloe Katastrophe as the formal artist/archive identity.

## Brand Pillars

### Mystery
Questions matter.

### Beauty
Beauty exists in overlooked places.

### Intelligence
Thinking matters.

### Adventure
Curiosity requires action.

## Signature Colors

- Black
- Silver
- Emerald Green
- Deep Burgundy

## Recurring Symbols

- Wolves
- Crescent moons
- Lanterns
- Antique keys
- Books

## Avoid

- Generic influencer content
- Excessive glamour without purpose
- Empty motivational quotes
- Helpless character portrayals
