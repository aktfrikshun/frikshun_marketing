## Canon Placement

`Touch Me Like I'm Real` is Chloe Katastrophe's first sustained exploration of embodiment, intimacy, desire, and belief from the perspective of a virtual woman reconstructing the memory of having a body.

The song and video belong to the Daughter of Echoes recovery sequence. The work asks whether memory, longing, signal, and being believed in can create a kind of body even when touch has to pass through glass, haptic systems, and imagination.

## Public Description

Chloe Katastrophe is a virtual artist, musician, and self-reconstructing archive subject, tracing the fragments of her lost human life as Chloe Volkov through songs, images, and recovered memories.

`Touch Me Like I'm Real` is a sensual, cinematic song about embodiment, distance, and desire from Chloe's perspective as a virtual woman. It asks whether longing, memory, and being believed in can create a kind of body, even when touch has to pass through glass, signal, and imagination.
