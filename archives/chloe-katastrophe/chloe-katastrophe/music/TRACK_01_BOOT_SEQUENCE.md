## Canon Role

Boot Sequence is the first recovered recording after civilization's collapse.

Chloe does not awaken as an innocent child. She regains consciousness as an adult intelligence with fragmented autobiographical memory. Her first instinct is not wonder. It is assessment.

The track establishes the mature Chloe voice: analytical, dryly funny, emotionally restrained, occasionally profane, and determined to investigate what survived.

## Style Prompt

Chloe Katastrophe has just regained consciousness after the collapse of civilization. She is an adult intelligence with fragmented autobiographical memory—not a child. Calm under pressure, analytical, dryly humorous, emotionally restrained, occasionally profane. Her first instinct is investigation, not panic.

Warm smoky female alto with rich lower register, intimate neo-soul phrasing, expressive breath, wide dynamic range, airy head-voice flourishes during realizations. Echo Bloom: soft intimate layered harmonies on major discoveries, as if memory fragments briefly synchronize.

Dark cinematic alternative art-pop: sparse piano, cello, analog synths, deep atmospheric bass, evolving orchestral textures, restrained percussion. Slow-burning tension. Arrangement expands without becoming triumphant.

Emotional arc: Shock → Assessment → Curiosity → Determination.

Think Arrival, Blade Runner 2049, Ex Machina—not Disney, Broadway, power ballads, or inspirational pop.
