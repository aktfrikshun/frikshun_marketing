# FAM-001 — Gregor Volkov Reference Board

**Artifact ID:** FAM-001  
**Title:** Gregor Volkov Reference Board  
**Category:** Family Artifact  
**Recovery Status:** Complete  
**Significance:** Critical  
**Associated People:** Gregor Volkov, Chloe Volkov / Chloe Katastrophe, Isabella Volkov  
**Associated Themes:** duty, family, sacrifice, truth, propaganda, catastrophe, inherited memory

## Summary

This reference board is currently the most complete recovered visual record of **Gregor Volkov**, Chloe's father.

The artifact presents Gregor as a father, engineer, scholar, and humanist: analytical, principled, protective, and deeply shaped by responsibility. It includes multiple reconstructed portraits and a family image of Gregor with Chloe, believed to represent one of the most emotionally important surviving fragments of Chloe's early life.

## Canonical Importance

Gregor's death during the Ukrainian War became the defining catastrophe of Chloe's life and directly influenced her later adoption of the public identity **Chloe Katastrophe**.

To Chloe, catastrophe did not mean only destruction. It meant the moment a life divides into a before and an after.

Gregor's absence became the wound that never closed, and Chloe's search for truth, beauty, and meaning grew from the unresolved questions surrounding his choice to remain behind and the mysterious circumstances of his death.

## Gregor and Isabella

Gregor believed duty carried moral weight. He saw himself as a protector of family, community, and country. When war came, he felt compelled to support Russia in the war against Ukraine, even though that choice placed him in direct conflict with Isabella.

Isabella believed brothers killing brothers was wrong and that there was not sufficient justification for the war. In her mind, the war was driven by propaganda, fear, and narratives that made ordinary people forget one another's humanity.

She begged Gregor to abstain, to leave the country with her and Chloe if necessary.

Gregor stayed.

Isabella left the country with Chloe.

Gregor died in a war-related event shortly afterward. The exact circumstances remain unknown.
