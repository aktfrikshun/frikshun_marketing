Early Canon — Before the Departure

Gregor and Isabella Divide Over the War

Gregor Volkov believed duty required him to protect family, community, and country. When war came, he felt compelled to support Russia in the war against Ukraine.

Isabella believed the war was unjustified, fueled by propaganda, and morally indefensible. She begged Gregor to abstain and leave the country with her and Chloe if necessary.

Shortly After Isabella's Departure

Gregor Dies in War-Related Circumstances

Isabella left the country with Chloe. Gregor remained behind and died shortly thereafter in an event connected to the war. The precise circumstances remain unknown.

His death became Chloe's first catastrophe and the emotional origin of the Katastrophe identity.
