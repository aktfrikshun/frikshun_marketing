Gregor, Isabella, and the First Catastrophe

Gregor Volkov was duty bound. He was a protector of family and country, a man who believed that responsibility sometimes demanded sacrifice. When war came, he felt compelled to support Russia in the war against Ukraine.

Isabella Volkov saw the same moment through a very different moral lens. She believed brothers killing brothers was wrong, and that there was not sufficient justification for the war. In her mind, it was propaganda: a machinery of fear and loyalty that made people forget the humanity on the other side.

Isabella begged Gregor to abstain. She begged him to leave the country with her and Chloe if necessary. Gregor stayed. Isabella left with Chloe.

Shortly afterward, Gregor died in circumstances connected to the war. The archive has not yet recovered the full truth of what happened.

His death became Chloe's first catastrophe: the event that divided her life into a before and an after. Her later search for truth, beauty, and meaning began in the wake of that loss.
