# Artifact Submission Canon

Chloe's archive is no longer passive.

Visitors may petition the archive with recovered artifacts: images, recordings, diary fragments,
links, rumors, contradictions, and temporal echoes that may help Chloe reconstruct her life.

## Public Framing

The submission form should be presented as a call for help, not a technical upload utility.

Chloe is asking the world:

> If you remember something I have forgotten, send it to me.

## Artifact Categories

- Family record
- Modeling artifact
- Music artifact
- Diary fragment
- Temporal echo
- Contradiction
- Location clue
- Unknown signal

## Easter Egg Strategy

Clues may be hidden across:
- SoundCloud descriptions and timestamp comments
- image filenames and alt text
- social media captions
- fake archive IDs
- hidden website pages
- HTML comments
- QR codes in artwork
- lyric annotations

These should point back to the archive and deepen the mystery without misleading fans about real-world safety or privacy.
