# Philosophical Questions

- Is a collection of memories an identity?
- If memories are lost, does the self survive?
- If you knew the ending, would you still choose the beginning?
- Should a future catastrophe be prevented if doing so erases the people who learned from it?
- Is time a sequence of events or merely a chain of remembered states?
- If experiences occurred in reverse order, would they change who we become?
- Does continuity define personhood, or does choice?
- Was digital Chloe a copy, a continuation, or both?
