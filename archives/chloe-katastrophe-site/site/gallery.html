Family Photographs

The Volkov family records are among the most emotionally significant areas of the archive. The Gregor-and-Chloe image is treated as a primary artifact because it anchors Chloe's later search for truth, sacrifice, and memory.

Family Record

Gregor Volkov

Russian by birth. Duty-bound, protective, disciplined. He believed family and country demanded sacrifice. His decision to remain behind during the war became the wound Chloe spent her life trying to understand.

Family Record

Isabella Volkov

Italian heritage. Passionate, empathetic, morally defiant. She believed the war was propaganda-fueled and begged Gregor to leave with her and Chloe. Her choice was survival, not surrender.

Family Record

Chloe Volkov

Child of duty and refusal, wolf and flame. Later known publicly as Chloe Katastrophe.
