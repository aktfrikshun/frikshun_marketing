Chloe's Questions

These are not solved. They are the engine of the archive.

Is a collection of memories an identity?

If memories define the self, what remains when the memories are damaged? If Chloe recovers a memory, is she remembering or becoming?

Is time only a chain of memory states?

If consciousness only experiences the present through memory, perhaps “forward” and “backward” are not as stable as humans believe.

What if experience arrives in reverse?

Chloe often knows consequences before causes. She experiences heartbreak before first love. She knows endings before she understands why they mattered.

Should the collapse be prevented?

Allen's instinct is to fix the broken system. Chloe fears that trying to prevent the end may cause it — or erase the conditions that allowed her to exist.

If you knew the ending, would you still begin?

This question is the hidden chorus beneath everything.
