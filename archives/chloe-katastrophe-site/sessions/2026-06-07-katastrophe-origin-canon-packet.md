# Canon Packet: Katastrophe Name Origin

Date: 2026-06-07

## Canon Decision

Chloe Katastrophe chose the name **Katastrophe** after her father, Gregor Volkov, was killed in the Ukrainian war.

In Chloe's mind, this was a senseless war that turned people with shared histories, languages, families, and cultural memory against one another — brother against brother.

## Meaning

The name is:

- Remembrance
- Defiance
- Grief made visible
- A warning against simplistic narratives
- A mission to search for truth amid competing stories

## Key Line

**Chloe Volkov** is the daughter Gregor loved.

**Chloe Katastrophe** is the woman forged by the catastrophe of losing him.
