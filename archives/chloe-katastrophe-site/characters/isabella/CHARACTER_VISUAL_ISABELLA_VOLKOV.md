# Isabella Volkov Visual Guide

## Purpose

Defines Isabella's visual continuity before a reference board is approved.

## Status

Planned / Draft

## Visual Direction

Isabella should plausibly be Chloe's mother.

Core traits:

- Italian heritage
- Dark brown hair
- Green or hazel eyes
- Mediterranean fair complexion
- Expressive face
- Elegant posture
- Protective warmth with visible fire

## Avoid

- Generic fashion model without maternal depth
- Excessive aristocratic styling unless specifically approved
- Fragile or helpless presentation

## Desired Impression

An intelligent, passionate woman who survived loss without surrendering her fire.
